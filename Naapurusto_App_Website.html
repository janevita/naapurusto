Key Features

Everything you need to stay connected to the place you actually live.

📍

Location-First Feed

Your feed is filtered by geography, not by who you follow. See what's happening on your street, in your district, or across your city — you control the radius.

🎙️

Multi-Format Content

Post text, record voice memos, share photos, or publish short videos. All content is size-limited to keep the feed fast, focused, and easy to read.

🕵️

Anonymous Reporting

File complaints, safety reports, and civic issues without revealing who you are. Anonymity protects your identity — not harmful content, which is always moderated.

📱

Cross-Platform

Full experience on iOS, Android, and web browser. Community kiosks — public touchscreen terminals — are planned for Phase 3 to extend access beyond smartphones.

🏅

Verified Local Badges

Active, trusted community members earn a location badge. Badged users get priority placement in feeds and additional credibility with their neighbors.

💬

Community Topics

Beyond the main feed, users can create and join topic threads — recurring conversations about specific local issues, events, or neighborhood interests.

Platform design

Built for every screen

Naapurusto works beautifully on mobile, tablet, and desktop. The neighborhood feed adapts to whatever device you're using.

📱 Mobile Feed
🗺️ Map View
✏️ Create Post

The main neighborhood feed shows posts from within your chosen radius, ordered by recency and local relevance. Each post shows the author's distance from you, their trust badge, and the post format.

The map view shows all recent posts as pins on a local map. Tap any pin to read the post. Filter by content type — reports, business promos, news, or community tips. Zoom out to see a wider area.

The post creation screen lets you choose your format (text, voice, photo, video, or link), add a topic tag, pick your radius, and optionally post anonymously. Character limits keep posts concise.

Business Model

Three revenue streams that mature as the community grows.

👤

Premium Profiles

Power users and local journalists upgrade to a premium tier with extended formats, analytics, and priority feed placement.

Subscription · $4.99/mo
📰

Sponsored Local Content

Local newspapers and media partners pay a monthly licensing fee to distribute branded stories and news segments into neighborhood feeds.

B2B Licensing · $500–2K/mo
🏪

Business Partnerships

Local shops and services promote geo-fenced offers to the people nearest them. Pay monthly flat-fee or per impression.

Local Ads · $49–199/mo
