Features

Everything your neighborhood needs

From breaking local news to anonymous issue reports — one app for the street you live on.

🏠
Hyperlocal Feed
See only what's happening in your neighborhood. No algorithm noise — just your street, your news.
🚨
Anonymous Reporting
Report local issues — potholes, graffiti, safety hazards — without revealing your identity. Your voice, protected.
💡
Ideas & Proposals
Propose improvements, gather community support, and track ideas all the way to city council action.
🗺
Neighborhood Map
See every report, event, and story pinned to the map. Understand your neighborhood at a glance.
✅
Resolution Tracking
See what happens after you report an issue. Track status updates from open to solved — accountability built in.
🔒
Privacy First
GDPR-compliant from day one. Minimal data collection. Your location data is never sold to third parties.

How it works

From report to resolution in four steps

1
Post or Report
Share news, flag an issue, propose an idea, or announce an event — text, photo, or voice.
2
Neighbors Rally
Your neighbors vote, comment, and add context. Shared priorities rise to the top.
3
Escalate to Council
High-support issues and ideas are forwarded to city council with the community's backing.
4
Track Resolution
Follow the issue through budget approval, implementation, and final resolution — all in the app.
